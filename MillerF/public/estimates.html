Cost Comparison: Internal Data Center vs. Cloud Services


Internal Data Center Costs

  • Hardware and infrastructure procurement
  • Facilities (electricity, cooling)
  • Staffing (administrators, maintenance)
  • Licensing and software
  • Real estate for server storage space
  • Productivity loss during downtime
  • Depreciation of hardware
  • Disaster recovery measures

Cloud Services Costs

  • Subscription fees for compute, storage,
    and networking
  • Data transfer and storage costs
  • Support and maintenance fees
  • Additional services (backup, security)
