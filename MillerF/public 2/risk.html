BENEFITS AND RISKS OF CLOUD MIGRATION

BENEFITS OF CLOUD MIGRATION

  • Cost savings on hardware and maintenance
  • Scalability and flexibility in resource allocation
  • Improved accessibility and collaboration

RISKS OF CLOUD MIGRATION

  • Data security and privacy concerns

    • Moving to the cloud requires an expansion of trust boundaries to include the external cloud. Overlapping of trust boundaries from different cloud customers and increased exposure of data can cause vulnerabilities for attack.



  • Reduced operational governance control

    • Cloud consumers are typically allotted lower governance control than for on-premise IT resources. Cloud providers could also be unreliable and not follow the Service Level Agreements (SLAs), affecting the quality of their services. Large location differences between the consumer and provider can also create latency and bandwidth constraints. For all of these reasons, it is important that cloud consumers keep track of the service they are receiving to ensure their requirements are being met.



  • Limited portability between cloud providers

    • Portability refers to the ease of moving a cloud system or IT resources from one infrastructure to another. This takes into account interoperability, which is the capacity of different cloud systems to interact with each other through their technologies and interfaces.
